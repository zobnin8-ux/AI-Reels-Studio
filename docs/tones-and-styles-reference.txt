--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. WHY EVERYTHING CAN FEEL LIKE "ONE TEMPLATE"
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Mood selector does not feed into image enrichPromptForGeneration.
  - Shared blocks in enrichPromptForGeneration (Lighting, Composition, Typography
    constraints) are the same across styles and can dominate the short style line.
  - portraLifestyle is missing a case in styleBlock — behaves like generic default.
